The Roman Colosseum

The Roman Colosseum was a public structure made by the Romans to entertain the lower class. The Colosseum was a arena where contents would fight other people, animals, or they would be executed. This building is significant because it resembles the morales and standards of the Roman people. It shows what they enjoyed/believed in and what activites they did. The Colosseum consisted of an all day schedule schedule of shows. The Colosseum could hold many people so it provided something to do for lots of bored romans.
